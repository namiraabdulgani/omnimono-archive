One Day on Earth

One Day on Earth is the first film made entirely by an online community. It is as much a video database and online community as it is a feature film experience. The result is a film reminiscent of Baraka that creates a beautiful cinematic portrait of planet earth in one day while telling the stories of the people who shot their day on 10/10/10. Two other films have been shot on 11/11/11 and 12/12/12.

In addition to doing the sound design and audio post-production for this film, we had the supreme joy of composing the music montage of the film. This diverse musical sound design is a film montage created by collaging audio from over thirty hours of music-related One Day on Earth footage captured all around the world on October 10th, 2010. The end result is a cinematic musical score that transports us around the world.
